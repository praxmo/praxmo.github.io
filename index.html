Intwixt delivers the Next Generation Workflow and Business Process Innovation

Conversational

Conversational means maintaining the context of the conversation and the ability to redirect based on past interaction across systems, people, and time. We enable more than just task management. Our workflows and business processes are conversational and able to interact with systems and people through natural language processing. Our approach is based on a conversation model that enables autonomous, distributed BPM (Business Process Management) modules to integrate and collaborate.

Cognitive

Cognitive computing uses artificial intelligence(AI) technologies to simulate human thought processes and conversations. Leveraging AI to transform workflows and business processes is an essential part of the Digital Transformation. Our cognitive workflows and business processes are using AI to sense, respond, predict, and learn, and further automate business process execution. This allows to go beyond rule-based workflows and business processes, making operations more fluid and flexible.

Stateful

In a stateless system, you are interacting with a limited system and a simple workflow task. Stateless interactions fail to support the essentially multi-step character of conversational business interactions and processes. A conversation in form of a business process spans systems , people, and time. It therefore needs to be stateful to perform efficiently. Our workflows and business processes are self-aware of their state and can maintain a conversation across systems, people, and time.

Model-driven

Our platform is model-based and designed for business users. It allows to manage software complexity, improve quality, and reduce development cost in a high-productivity application Platform-as-a-Service environment. Through declarative application construction in our highly scalable environment we can implement and integrate any workflow and business process. .
