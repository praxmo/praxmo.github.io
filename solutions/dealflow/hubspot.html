Intwixt DealFlow
HubSpot Setup

I. Click the Authorize Button

Once you install DealFlow to your Slack workspace, you will receive a Slack message, prompting you to authorize access to HubSpot.

II. Choose the HubSpot Instance

A new browser window will open, prompting you to choose which HubSpot instance to authorize.

III. Click the Grant Access Button

You will be redirected to the authorization page, showing which permissions are being requested.

You will be redirected to a "success" page, which you can close.

You will see a message appear in Slack, alerting you that HubSpot authorization was successfully configured. HubSpot setup is now complete!
