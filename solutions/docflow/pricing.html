DocFlow Pricing

Teams are assigned to the free Intro Plan when DocFlow is first installed to their Slack workspace. Upgrade to a paid plan to run at scale. The Standard Plan is priced according to the number of Slack team members who will need to submit documents (submitters). Ask us about our Enterprise Plan if you're interested in your own customized instance, volume pricing, or require specific support options.
